  • 4 Barriers: Awareness, consideration, and distance
    • 4.1 Description and relevance to Effective Giving: Typically, the most effective (humanitarian) charity will go from wealthy countries in the “North” to poor countries in the “South”. The large, social, cultural geographic distance is an important barrier to giving and a reason why people prefer to give locally. However, technology continues to reduce these barriers (see, e.g., Give Directly and their new web-enabled tools).
